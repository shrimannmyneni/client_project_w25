Organizations on Campus

Here are some student organizations at the University of Michigan.

Society of Physics Students

The Society of Physics Students promotes a collaborative environment for students passionate about physics & other sciences.

Four students from the Society of Physics Students smiling and holding a handmade sign at UMichigan Festifall.

Students for Clean Energy

Students for Clean Energy advocates for sustainable practices and renewable energy initiatives on campus.

A student from Students for Clean Energy talking to a prospective member at Festifall, standing next to a display board showcasing the club initiatives.

Multicultural Organizations on Campus

These are some multicultural organizations at the University of Michigan.

Black Student Union

The Black Student Union fosters a strong sense of community and support for Black students at the University of Michigan.

Students engaging with the Black Student Union booth at Festifall, beside an informational display board about the club.

Filipino Student Association

The Filipino Student Association promotes Filipino culture and heritage while fostering a vibrant community for its members.

A member of the Filipino Student Association speaking with students at Festifall, standing beside a display board showcasing past group activities.

Black Students for Aerospace

Black Students for Aerospace provides resources and mentorship for Black students pursuing a degree and/or careers in aerospace engineering.

Two students representing Black Students for Aerospace at Festifall, standing next to a table with an informational board, promotional materials, and a laptop.
