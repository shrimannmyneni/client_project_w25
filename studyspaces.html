Study Spaces on Campus

Featured Study Spots

The University of Michigan features many spots on campus to study, ranging from small to large, and from quiet to loud. Here are some iconic spots featured below:

Quieter Locations on Campus

You may not hear even a whisper.

Hatcher Stacks Reference Room

The Hatcher Stacks Reference Room is known for its quiet environment, perfect for focused and uninterrupted studying.

Students study in the Hatcher Stacks Reference Room.

Law Library

The Law Library, with its grand architecture and hushed atmosphere, provides one of the quietest and most aesthetic study spots on campus.

Students study in the Law Library.

Louder Locations on Campus

You may never not hear someone talking here.

Ross School of Business

The Ross School of Business offers a collaborative atmosphere, ideal for group discussions and collaborative projects.

Students study in the Ross School of Business.

Chemistry Building

The Chemistry Building is a popular choice, with its large stone steps and open spaces that encourage a mix of studying and socializing.

Students study in the Chemistry Building.
